Our Ai Robots

"Once upon a time, in the world of crypto, five unique trading bots were created to assist traders in generating profits of up to 35% per month!

Crazy Horse

Fearless robot that loved to chase market volatility.



Crazy Horse was the first of the five, a fearless robot that loved to chase market volatility. Crazy Horse was programmed to buy the most promising currencies, with a focus on maximizing returns through resales. Despite its risky nature, Crazy Horse often earned its users an impressive 6% on resales.

Market Mirror

A robot that worked in perfect harmony with the market.



Next came Market Mirror, a sophisticated robot that worked in perfect harmony with the market. If the market was low, Market Mirror would adjust its strategy accordingly, and if the market was high, it would operate in a different way. Market Mirror was the go-to robot for experienced traders who wanted to stay ahead of the game.

Fast Trade

Buying and selling currencies at lightning speed.



Fast Trade was a robot that lived up to its name, buying and selling currencies at lightning speed. This robot operated more frequently than any of the others, but with that came a higher risk of loss. Despite this, Fast Trade was the perfect choice for those looking to make quick gains, with a 1.1% profit margin and the ability to day trade.

Wallet Manager

A more laid-back robot, with a focus on long-term investments.



Wallet Manager was a more laid-back robot, with a focus on long-term investments. This robot bought a minimum of four coins, combining all investments into a single portfolio. Wallet Manager sought out small gains, but over time, these gains added up to significant returns for its users. Bitcoin, Ethereum, BND, and Solana were Wallet Manager's favorite currencies to trade.

Terminator

Seeking out the best offer and buying from its wallet.



Last but not least, there was Terminator. Terminator was a robot with a mission, seeking out the best offer and buying from its wallet. Once it had made a purchase, it operated like Market Mirror, adapting its strategy to the market's movements. Terminator was the perfect choice for traders who wanted a combination of speed and sophistication in their trades.

H8121

Born and bred to win



Introducing the newest addition to the world of commercial robots: the H8121. Born and bred in the depths of EPCAR, this robot is a true masterpiece of programming and engineering. Through a process of continuous learning and adaptation, the H8121 (also known as Tudo AV) has become even more sophisticated than the Terminator.
